Built you **Desert Preflight** — a retro Palm Springs-style artwork rescue tool for print jobs.

Why it’s cool:
- Upload any image and instantly see whether it’s actually printable at the size you want
- Calculates effective DPI with a clear score meter
- Flags common issues like low resolution, aspect-ratio mismatch, missing bleed, and RGB-to-print caveats
- Generates a plain-English explanation you can copy straight to a client
- Gives a practical rescue plan instead of just saying “this file sucks”

This one feels stronger than pure whimsy — it could turn into a real Inspired Design lead-gen/preflight tool.
